Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Newzyy ("we", "us") respects your privacy. This page explains, plainly, what data we collect and why — no legal boilerplate for its own sake.

What we collect

  • Reading preferences: your dark/light mode choice and login state are sent to our - servers.
  • Article view counts: we record how many times an article is viewed, stored against the article itself, not against you personally.
  • Contact form submissions: if you email us or use the contact form, we keep your message and email address only to respond to you.

What we don't do

  • We don't sell your data to advertisers or anyone else.
  • We don't run third-party ad-tracking scripts on this site.
  • We don't require an account to read any article.

Third-party services we rely on

To gather and produce our news coverage, Newzyy's backend pulls articles by the help of "newzyy staffs" to write original articles from those facts. These are one-way data pulls for content.

Your choices

You can clear your browser's local storage at any time to remove your saved theme/login preference. Contact us at our contact page if you'd like any data we hold about you removed.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes materially, we'll update the date above and, for significant changes, note it on our homepage.
